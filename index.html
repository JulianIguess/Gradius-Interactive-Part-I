Gradius Interactive Part I

Welcome, rookie. You are a pilot of the Vic Viper, a legendary fighter starship. An evil alien army known as the Bacterian has invaded the peaceful planet known as Gradius. Not only have they invaded this peaceful planet, but they had also invaded other planets, too. Your mission is to go through the invaded planets, destroy the Bacterian bases, and head to planet Gradius to destroy them all!

It turns out the Bacterian put an impenetrable barrier around Gradius. If you can destroy the bases on other planets, then you can make the barrier disappear.

There are two planets, each with a Bacterian base. Where will you go first? The Metalion, another fighter starship, wil take care of the planet you didn't choose.

You head to the Volcanic Planet, and it’s a big mess! Not only have you got to deal with Bacterian forces, but a cavalcade of flaming debris is heading toward you!

You dodged the coming debris, as it then grows and grows. One had hit your ship’s right wing and you couldn’t control it! You had crashed into a sea of fire.

You start to shoot oncoming debris, and they split into thirds! Luckily, you destroyed the debris and have come across a Bacterian base.

Wyvern, the three-headed fire dragon, is guarding it! You manage to destroy the dragon and the Bacterian base, and you flee the scorching planet.

You blast off to the Plant Planet, a planet thriving of beautiful plant life. The scenery is outstanding, but you can’t look now, there’s bases nearby!

An army of Bacterian is ready to aim and shoot you down. But you find a strange blue Power Capsule that could probably help you. What will you do?

You take down the Bacterian head-on, as they relentlessly fire! The incoming fire is too much, and when you weren’t looking, an enemy had shot your ship’s engine! Your ship gets destroyed, along with you. They have won..

You collect the blue Power Capsule, glowing with energy. You try to find out what it does, and then it blasts energy all around the area, eliminating all Bacterian forces in your sight.

You found a Bacterian base, but a giant ship known as Big Core is in your way! Luckily, you destroyed the ship and the Bacterian base, and leave the planet of flora.

The Bacterian bases are destroyed. You and the Metalion did a good job.

This isn't good. The Bacterian had installed another base on another planet! That means the Bacterian's defenses on Gradius won't go down!

We have to go to this third planet and take out the new base there! Let's go!
